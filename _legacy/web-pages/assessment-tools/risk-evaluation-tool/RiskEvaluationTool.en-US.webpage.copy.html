Risk evaluation tool

Systematic assessment of re-identification risks, privacy vulnerabilities, and threat scenarios for synthetic health data projects. This tool helps quantify and prioritise risks to guide effective mitigation strategies.

Framework Context: This tool supports Step 4 (Assess Re-identification Risks) and ongoing risk monitoring throughout your project lifecycle.
Important: Complete this evaluation after synthetic data generation (Step 3) but before final deployment. Results inform your risk mitigation planning in Step 5.

Framework integration and resources

This risk evaluation tool integrates directly with the SynD-DGF framework to provide comprehensive guidance and ensure compliance with privacy obligations.

Using your risk assessment results

Interpreting risk levels

  • Low Risk: Standard safeguards sufficient, routine monitoring recommended
  • Medium Risk: Additional controls needed, enhanced monitoring required
  • High Risk: Comprehensive mitigation essential, frequent review necessary
  • Critical Risk: Project may not be suitable for synthetic data approach

Next steps

  • Use results to develop targeted risk mitigation strategies
  • Implement recommended technical and procedural controls
  • Establish ongoing risk monitoring processes
  • Document risk decisions for regulatory compliance
  • Review and update assessments regularly
