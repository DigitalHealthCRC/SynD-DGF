Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to implement the SynD-DGF framework?
Implementation timelines vary depending on organisational complexity and existing governance structures. Typically, basic implementation takes 3-6 months, while comprehensive implementation with full integration may take 6-12 months. We provide guidance to help organisations plan realistic timelines.
Is there a cost for framework implementation support?
Basic support including access to tools, resources, and email guidance is provided free of charge. Intensive implementation support, training workshops, and consulting services may involve fees. We work with organisations to find cost-effective solutions that meet their needs and budgets.
Can the framework be adapted for specific industry sectors?
Yes, the framework is designed to be adaptable across different healthcare sectors and contexts. We provide guidance on customisation for specific settings such as public hospitals, research institutions, digital health companies, and government health departments.
How do you ensure cultural safety in Indigenous health data governance?
Our approach to Indigenous health data governance follows the CARE principles and incorporates guidance from Indigenous data sovereignty experts. We provide specific cultural protocols and consultation processes to ensure appropriate engagement and respect for Indigenous community rights and values.
Do you provide ongoing support after initial implementation?
Yes, we offer various forms of ongoing support including updates to the framework, access to a community of practice, periodic check-ins, troubleshooting assistance, and guidance on emerging issues or regulatory changes.
Can international organisations use the SynD-DGF framework?
While the framework is designed for the Australian regulatory context, many principles and tools are applicable internationally. We provide guidance on adapting the framework for different regulatory environments and can connect international organisations with local experts.
