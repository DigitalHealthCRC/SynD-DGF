Appendix 8: Decision tree for complex scenarios

Interactive decision support for navigating complex synthetic health data scenarios that fall outside standard framework implementation.

This appendix provides structured guidance for multi-organisation arrangements, complex data sharing scenarios, third-party synthetic data generation, and other non-standard implementations requiring additional consideration and safeguards.

Complex scenario examples

Multi-state hospital network data sharing High Complexity

Scenario: A hospital network operating across Victoria, NSW, and Queensland wants to create synthetic datasets for cross-state health service planning research.

Key considerations:

  • Legal framework alignment: Different privacy laws apply in each jurisdiction
  • Data governance: Multiple organisational policies and procedures
  • Indigenous data sovereignty: Different Traditional Owner groups across regions
  • Technical harmonisation: Consistent data standards and synthetic data generation methods
  • Risk management: Coordinated risk assessment and mitigation strategies
Required assessments:
  • Legal framework analysis for each jurisdiction
  • Multi-organisation impact assessment
  • Cross-border data transfer evaluation
  • Coordinated safety assessment
Decision support tools:
  • Legal Pathways Wizard
  • Jurisdiction Mapper
  • Risk Mitigation Planner
Third-party synthetic data generation service Medium Complexity

Scenario: A regional health service contracts an external analytics company to generate synthetic datasets for internal quality improvement projects.

Key considerations:

  • Data processor agreements: Clear contractual obligations for synthetic data generation
  • Technical oversight: Validation of synthetic data generation methods and quality
  • Privacy safeguards: Additional protections for external data processing
  • Return/destruction: Secure handling of source data and intermediate outputs
  • Audit requirements: Ongoing monitoring and compliance verification
Required assessments:
  • Technical assessment of external capabilities
  • Enhanced safety assessment for external processing
  • Contractual and legal framework review
  • Data processor due diligence
Additional safeguards:
  • Data processing agreements with specific synthetic data clauses
  • Technical validation of generation methods
  • Regular audit and monitoring requirements
  • Secure data transfer and storage protocols
Commercial health analytics platform High Complexity

Scenario: A health tech startup wants to create synthetic datasets for developing and marketing a commercial health analytics platform to international markets.

Key considerations:

  • Public benefit requirement: Demonstrating clear health system benefits beyond commercial gain
  • International compliance: Meeting privacy requirements across target markets
  • Data licensing: Appropriate licensing agreements for synthetic data use
  • Transparency obligations: Clear disclosure of synthetic data use in commercial products
  • Ongoing governance: Sustained oversight of commercial data applications
⚠️ Additional requirements for commercial scenarios

Commercial applications require enhanced scrutiny and additional safeguards including:

  • Explicit demonstration of public health benefit
  • Enhanced transparency and disclosure requirements
  • Ongoing monitoring of commercial data use
  • Clear data licensing and use limitation agreements
  • Regular review of continued public benefit delivery
