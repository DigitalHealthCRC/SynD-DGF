Assessment workflow guidance

1

Start with core assessments

Complete both the Use Case Assessment Checklist and Impact Assessment before proceeding with your project. These determine whether your project can proceed under the framework and must address all three mandatory tests and comprehensive impact considerations.

2

Document assessment outcomes

Use the Assessment Outcomes and Documentation tool to formally record your assessment results, decisions, and rationale. This creates the official record for accountability and compliance monitoring.

3

Use specialised tools as needed

Apply the Risk Evaluation Tool and Quality Metrics Tool at appropriate framework steps to ensure comprehensive risk management and quality assurance throughout your project lifecycle.

4

Maintain ongoing compliance

Use the Compliance Checklist Tool throughout your project lifecycle to track regulatory requirements, governance obligations, and ensure continuous adherence to framework standards.
