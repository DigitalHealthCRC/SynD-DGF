About SynD-DGF

The Synthetic Data Governance Framework (SynD-DGF) provides comprehensive guidance for navigating legal and technical challenges in synthetic health data generation and use in Australia.

About the framework

The SynD-DGF addresses critical gaps in governance approaches for synthetic health data, providing practical guidance for organisations seeking to harness the benefits of synthetic data while maintaining robust privacy protections and regulatory compliance.

Key features

  • 5-step systematic process for synthetic data governance
  • Interactive assessment tools and decision support
  • Comprehensive legal and ethical guidance
  • Indigenous data sovereignty considerations
  • Practical implementation resources

Development methodology

The framework was developed through rigorous research methodology combining legal analysis, technical evaluation, and extensive stakeholder consultation to ensure practical applicability and regulatory compliance.

Development approach

  • Comprehensive legal and regulatory analysis
  • International best practice review
  • Multi-stakeholder consultation process
  • Iterative design and testing
  • Expert review and validation

Stakeholder consultation

Extensive consultation with health organisations, researchers, regulators, technology providers, and community representatives ensures the framework meets real-world needs and reflects diverse perspectives.

Consultation participants

  • Technology and industry partners
  • Community and patient representatives
