Record keeping and compliance requirements

Mandatory record keeping

Organisations must maintain comprehensive records for the following minimum periods:

  • Assessment documentation: 7 years from completion date
  • Decision records: 7 years from decision date
  • Consultation evidence: 5 years from project completion
  • Compliance monitoring: Duration of project plus 3 years
  • Indigenous sovereignty documentation: As specified by relevant Indigenous groups or permanent retention

Ongoing compliance monitoring

Approved projects must maintain ongoing compliance through:

  • Annual compliance reviews and reporting
  • Monitoring of community feedback and concerns
  • Regular review of risk mitigation effectiveness
  • Updates to reflect changes in legal or policy frameworks
  • Periodic reassessment if project scope or use cases change

Audit and review requirements

Assessment outcomes may be subject to:

  • Internal audit and quality assurance review
  • External regulatory or oversight body examination
  • Community or stakeholder group review
  • Academic or expert peer review
  • Legal or compliance audit
