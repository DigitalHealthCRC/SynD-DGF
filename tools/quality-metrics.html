Quality metrics tool

Comprehensive evaluation of synthetic data quality across statistical fidelity, utility preservation, and privacy protection dimensions. This tool provides quantitative metrics and qualitative assessments to ensure your synthetic data meets requirements for intended use.

Framework Context: Use this tool during Step 3 (Generate Synthetic Data) to validate quality before proceeding to Step 4 risk assessment.
Best Practice: Conduct quality evaluation iteratively during synthetic data generation to guide parameter tuning and method selection.

Quality improvement guidance

Interpreting quality scores

  • Excellent (90-100%): Synthetic data meets highest quality standards
  • Good (75-89%): High quality with minor areas for improvement
  • Acceptable (60-74%): Adequate quality but benefits from enhancement
  • Poor (<60%): Quality issues require significant attention

Common improvement strategies

  • Parameter tuning for generation algorithms
  • Enhanced preprocessing of source data
  • Post-processing to improve specific metrics
  • Ensemble methods combining multiple approaches
  • Iterative refinement based on quality feedback
